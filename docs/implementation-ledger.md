# Implementation And Review Ledger

This ledger is the review surface for incremental AMFlow-port batches. Every implementation batch should be recorded here with its scope, verification evidence, and second-pass review status before it is treated as accepted.

## Entry Template

| Batch | Scope | Owner | Reviewer | Status | Verification | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Batch N` | short summary | implementer | reviewer | `planned`, `implemented`, `reviewed`, or `changes-requested` | exact commands, success/failure, and the targeted artifact or behavior confirmed | follow-ups |

Notes must record the second-pass disposition explicitly once it runs, for example
`second-pass: approved` or `second-pass: changes-requested`.
The latest reviewed batch note should also carry the next-batch recommendation explicitly.
Global scheduling is governed by the `R0` track scheduler in
`docs/full-amflow-completion-roadmap.md`; per-row next-batch recommendations are local suggestions
inside the active track, not the authoritative global order.
