# Finite Fields And Reconstruction

This section exists for two reasons:

- Kira 2/3 and Blade rely on finite-field machinery directly or indirectly.
- A future rewrite may want its own reconstruction or black-box infrastructure.

The most important items here are:

- Peraro 2016 on finite-field reconstruction
- FiniteFlow 2019
- FireFly 2020 and 2021

For V1 of a port, these are reference material, not necessarily reimplementation targets.
